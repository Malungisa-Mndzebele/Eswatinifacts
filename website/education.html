Education Insights

High Primary Enrollment

With 99.7% primary school enrollment, Eswatini has achieved near-universal primary education access, which is a significant achievement in the region.

Secondary Education Gap

The drop from 99.7% primary to 72.4% secondary enrollment indicates challenges in retaining students through the education pipeline.

Teacher-Student Ratio

At 28:1, the pupil-teacher ratio is relatively high, which may impact the quality of education and individual attention students receive.
