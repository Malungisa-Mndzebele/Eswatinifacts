Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

How often is your data updated?

We update our data quarterly, pulling from the latest available sources including World Bank, IMF, and official Eswatini government statistics.

Can I use your data in my research?

Yes! All our data is freely available for research, educational, and journalistic purposes. Please cite Eswatini Facts as your source.

Do you accept data corrections?

Absolutely. If you find an error in our data, please report it to issues@eswatinifacts.org with supporting documentation.

Can I request specific data analysis?

We welcome data requests! While we can't fulfill every request, we prioritize those that serve public interest and align with our mission.

How do you ensure data accuracy?

We cross-reference multiple authoritative sources, verify data with original publications, and maintain transparency about our methodology.

Is your work politically neutral?

Yes. We present data objectively without political bias. Our mission is to make information accessible, not to advocate for specific policies.
