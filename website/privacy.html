Privacy Policy

How we handle your information

Last updated: June 2026

Eswatini Facts ("we", "us") is a nonpartisan, data-driven information project. This policy explains what information we collect, why, and the choices you have. We aim to collect as little personal data as possible.

What We Collect

Information You Give Us

  • Your name, email and message when you use the contact or volunteer forms
  • Your email if you subscribe to updates
  • Any details you choose to include in a message

Information Collected Automatically

  • Anonymised usage data via Google Analytics — only if you accept analytics cookies
  • Standard server logs (such as IP address) kept by our hosting provider

What We Do Not Collect

  • We do not sell or rent your personal data
  • We do not run advertising or ad-tracking networks
  • We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13

Cookies & Analytics

We use Google Analytics (GA4) to understand how the site is used. Analytics cookies are only set after you click "Accept" on our cookie banner; if you decline, Google Analytics stays disabled. You can change your mind by clearing this site's data in your browser, which will bring the banner back. Essential page functionality works without any analytics cookies.

How We Use Your Information

To Respond

  • Reply to your enquiries and volunteer applications
  • Send updates you have asked for

To Improve

  • Understand which content is useful
  • Diagnose problems and improve the site

Third-Party Services

  • Google Analytics & Google Fonts
  • YouTube (for embedded videos)
  • Form/email delivery services and our hosting provider

These providers process data under their own privacy policies. Embedded YouTube videos may set cookies when you play them.

Your Rights

You can ask us to access, correct or delete the personal information you have shared with us, or unsubscribe at any time. Contact info@eswatinifacts.com and we will respond as promptly as we can.

Data Retention

We keep messages and subscription details only as long as needed for the purpose you contacted us about, or until you ask us to delete them.

Changes

We may update this policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected by the "last updated" date above.

Contact

Questions about privacy? Email info@eswatinifacts.com or use our contact form. See also our Terms of Use.
