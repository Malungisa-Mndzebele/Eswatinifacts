Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about joining our team

Do I need to be based in Eswatini?

No! While we welcome local contributors, we also work with people from around the world who are passionate about Eswatini's development and data transparency.

Is this a paid position?

Currently, all positions are volunteer-based. However, we provide recognition, skill development, networking opportunities, and the satisfaction of contributing to an important cause.

How much time do I need to commit?

Time commitments vary by role, from 1-2 hours per week for some tasks to 10+ hours for more intensive projects. We're flexible and work around your schedule.

What if I don't have technical skills?

We need people with all kinds of skills! Research, writing, translation, community outreach, and many other non-technical roles are just as valuable.

Can students participate?

Absolutely! Students bring fresh perspectives and energy. This is also a great way to build your portfolio and gain real-world experience.

How do you ensure quality?

All contributions go through a review process. We provide training, guidelines, and feedback to help maintain high standards while supporting your growth.
