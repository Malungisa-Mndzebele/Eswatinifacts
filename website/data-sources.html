Methodology

Data Collection

We collect data from official government sources, international organizations, and reputable research institutions. All data is verified through multiple sources when possible.

Data Processing

Raw data is cleaned, standardized, and processed to ensure consistency across different sources and time periods. We maintain detailed documentation of all transformations.

Quality Assurance

All data undergoes rigorous quality checks including source verification, cross-referencing, and validation against known benchmarks and trends.

Updates and Maintenance

Data is updated regularly based on the availability of new information from source organizations. We maintain a schedule of regular updates and communicate changes transparently.

Transparency and Accountability

Source Attribution

Every data point is clearly attributed to its source with links to original documents and reports whenever possible.

Methodology Documentation

Our data collection and processing methods are fully documented and available for review by researchers and the public.

Error Reporting

We maintain a system for reporting and correcting errors. Users can report discrepancies through our contact system.

Regular Audits

Our data and methodology undergo regular internal and external audits to ensure accuracy and reliability.
