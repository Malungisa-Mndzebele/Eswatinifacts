Our Methodology

Data Collection & Verification

We collect data from official government sources, international organizations, and peer-reviewed publications. All data is cross-referenced and verified against multiple sources when possible.

Update Frequency

Data is updated as new information becomes available from source organizations. Most economic indicators are updated annually, while health and education data may be updated more frequently.

Data Quality

We prioritize data from official sources and international organizations with established methodologies. When discrepancies exist between sources, we note this in our analysis and use the most recent or most comprehensive data available.

Transparency

Every statistic on our website includes source attribution and links to the original data. Users can verify our findings and access the raw data for their own analysis.
