We comb through the data so you don't have to.

Our mission

Most people don’t spend their weekends buried in World Bank spreadsheets. We do — so you can get the facts about Eswatini without a decoder ring. We gather the numbers that matter, show exactly where each one comes from, and leave the conclusions to you.

Eswatini’s data is scattered across dozens of reports and portals, in formats few citizens have time to parse. We pull it together, cite it plainly, and keep it current — for students, journalists, policymakers, and any citizen who wants to understand their country.

How we work

Sourced

Every figure is tied to an authoritative, public source and dated. No number without a citation — see Sources & Method.

Nonpartisan

We present the data as it is, without political bias or agenda. Our job is to inform, not to influence.

Current

We update figures as new official data is released, and correct the record quickly when we get something wrong.
