Our Story

Eswatini Facts was born from a simple observation: while there's an abundance of data about Eswatini's development, it's often scattered across multiple sources, presented in complex formats, or buried in lengthy reports that are difficult for the average citizen to understand.

We believe that access to clear, accurate data is essential for informed decision-making and democratic participation. Whether you're a student researching Eswatini's development, a policymaker looking for evidence-based insights, or simply a citizen who wants to understand your country better, you deserve data that's accessible and understandable.

Our team of data analysts, researchers, and designers work together to transform complex datasets into clear, visual, and actionable insights. We don't just present numbers ‚Äì we provide context, analysis, and the tools you need to understand what the data means for Eswatini's future.
