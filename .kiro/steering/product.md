# Product Overview

Eswatini Facts is a USAFacts-inspired data transparency platform providing nonpartisan, data-driven insights about the Kingdom of Eswatini.

## Purpose

The platform serves as a comprehensive information resource covering:
- Economic indicators and analysis
- Healthcare statistics and trends
- Educational system data
- Political system and governance
- Demographics and cultural insights

## Target Audience

- Researchers and academics
- Journalists and media professionals
- Policy makers and government officials
- Students and educators
- General public interested in Eswatini

## Key Features

- Interactive data visualizations using Chart.js and Plotly
- Responsive design for all devices
- Video content integration from YouTube channel (@Eswatiniinfo)
- Contact and contribution forms
- Data sources transparency
- SEO-optimized content

## Brand Voice

- Nonpartisan and data-driven
- Transparent about sources and methodology
- Accessible and educational
- Professional yet approachable
